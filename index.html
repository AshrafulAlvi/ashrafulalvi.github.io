Who are we?

We are one and everyone. We see the world in a diifferent view. we have our own perspective. we capture the moment of happiness and sorrow and grief. Our camera speaks for our minds and thoughts. We share what is going on in and around us.

If you have a camera and a passion for photography than we would be glad to have you in our club as an official member. You don't need certification and or degree to be a part of our family. The great thing about our club is that we visit many tourist places every month. So, there is a great chance for you to be a part of this memories.

We do not discriminate between male, female, young, adult, professional or newbie. as long as you have something to take picture with than we will be more than happy to converge you into our family. There are no high value fee to be in our society. Actually it is completely free. All we ask from you is the contribution of self presense and the dedication.
